About Fei Lin

I'm Fei Lin—a makeup artist based in Irvine, originally from Taiwan. With years of experience in photoshoot styling. I specialize in working with Asian facial features and take pride in enhancing each client's natural beauty. I listen closely to your needs and ideas, offering thoughtful guidance to help bring your vision to life. To me, makeup isn't about transformation—it's about helping you feel confident, the best version of yourself.
